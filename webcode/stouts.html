Entry #1: The Cross River (upstream of its confluence with the Mitchell)



Scenic road to road put in and takeout. Runs off snowmelt late in the summer. Take the road towards nipika towards a quartz mine. Right before the mine there will be a bridge crossing the river with roads going up either side of the valley. The first is blown out and the second is narrow and scenic. We made it up with a Ford Focus but more clearance is nice.

Entry #4: Bobby burns falls



20-30 footer that is typically done in the middle of a two day multiday down the Bobby Burns. It can be park and hucked by following some FSRs and bushwacking a bit. As we learned, 80 is a bit on the high side and 40 is ideal, using the Spillimacheen gauge.
